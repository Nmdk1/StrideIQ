# Competitor Analysis: Runna.com

**Analysis Date:** January 5, 2026  
**Competitor:** Runna (https://www.runna.com/)

## Overview

Runna is a mobile-first running coaching app with strong market presence (76,000+ App Store ratings, 4.9 stars). They focus on personalized training plans with a freemium model.

## Pricing Structure

### Runna's Pricing
- **Free Plan**: Available (limited features)
- **Monthly**: $19.99/month
- **Annual**: $119.99/year ($9.99/month effective - **50% discount**)
- **First week free** trial

### Our Pricing
- **Free**: $0 - All calculators + basic insights
- **Fixed Plan**: $9/plan - One-time custom training plan
- **Race-Specific**: $12/plan - Race-targeted plan
- **Guided Self-Coaching**: $24/month - Premium adaptive coaching

## Key Differentiators Runna Has (That We Don't)

### 1. **Mobile App Presence**
- ✅ Native iOS and Android apps
- ✅ App Store and Google Play presence
- ✅ High ratings (4.9/5 on App Store, 4.7/5 on Play Store)
- ❌ **We have:** Web-only (no mobile apps)

### 2. **Race Time Calculator/Estimator**
- ✅ Interactive "Unlock Your Potential" calculator
- ✅ Predicts race times based on:
  - Current running level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced/Elite)
  - Training distance (5k/10k/Half/Marathon)
  - Recent race times
  - Training days per week
  - Plan duration
- ✅ Immediate results
- ❌ **We have:** Training pace calculator, but no race time predictor

### 3. **Social Proof & Reviews**
- ✅ Prominent display of ratings (76,000+ App Store, 17,000+ Play Store)
- ✅ User testimonials with names and photos
- ✅ "As featured in" section (Marie Claire, Runner's World, etc.)
- ✅ Review snippets throughout site
- ❌ **We have:** No social proof yet

### 4. **Free Trial Strategy**
- ✅ "First week free. Cancel anytime."
- ✅ Low barrier to entry
- ✅ Clear cancellation policy
- ❌ **We have:** Free calculators but no trial for premium

### 5. **Plan Types by Distance**
- ✅ Specific plans: 5k, 10k, Half Marathon, Marathon, Ultramarathon
- ✅ Clear navigation by goal distance
- ✅ Tailored messaging per distance
- ❌ **We have:** Generic "Fixed Plan" and "Race-Specific Plan"

### 6. **Visual Design Elements**
- ✅ Mobile app screenshots prominently displayed
- ✅ Multiple device mockups showing app in action
- ✅ Clean, modern UI with lots of white space
- ✅ Strong use of imagery (runners, app screens)
- ❌ **We have:** Text-heavy, fewer visuals

### 7. **Annual Pricing Discount**
- ✅ 50% discount for annual ($9.99/month vs $19.99/month)
- ✅ Clear savings messaging
- ✅ Encourages longer commitment
- ❌ **We have:** No annual pricing option

### 8. **Coach/Expert Positioning**
- ✅ "Our coaches" section
- ✅ Expert credibility
- ✅ Human element emphasized
- ❌ **We have:** AI-focused, less human touch

### 9. **Community/Events**
- ✅ "Runna Events" section
- ✅ Community aspect
- ✅ Race partnerships
- ❌ **We have:** No community features

### 10. **App Store Optimization**
- ✅ Strong presence on both platforms
- ✅ High ratings drive trust
- ✅ Easy download CTAs
- ❌ **We have:** Web-only, no app store presence

## Competitive Positioning

### Core Philosophical Difference

**Runna's Approach: Extract Value FROM Consumers**
- Requires app download to see value
- Free plan is limited (freemium model)
- Free trial requires credit card
- Mobile-first (forces app ecosystem)
- Social proof drives FOMO
- Annual discount locks you in

**Our Approach: Provide Value TO Consumers**
- All tools free, no signup required
- Instant value - use calculators immediately
- No barriers, no credit card for free tools
- Web-based (accessible everywhere)
- Research-backed, transparent formulas
- You own your data, no lock-in

### Runna's Strengths
- Mobile-first approach
- Strong brand recognition
- Social proof (ratings, reviews)
- Simple, clear value prop
- Free trial reduces friction

### Our Strengths
- **Value-first philosophy** - We give before we ask
- Superior free tools (no signup required)
- More advanced calculations
- Adaptive AI coaching (vs static plans)
- Better transparency
- Research-backed formulas
- **No barriers to value** - Use tools immediately

### Our Unique Value
- **Age-Grading** - WMA standards, not common
- **Adaptive Coaching** - Continuous learning vs static plans
- **Accessibility** - Web-based, no app download needed
- **Transparency** - All tools free, clear pricing
- **Value-first ethos** - We prove our competence before asking for payment
