---

## Purpose

Per Section 6.6 of the Garmin Connect Developer Program Agreement, this notice
provides 30 days' prior written notice of content formats and display mechanisms
in StrideIQ that incorporate Garmin-sourced data.

StrideIQ is a running intelligence platform that helps athletes understand their
training through personalized coaching insights. We display Garmin data to the
athlete who owns it, with proper Garmin Connect attribution on every screen.

---

## Display Formats

### 1. Home Page — Last Run Card with Mini Pace Chart

**Data displayed:** Activity name, distance, duration, pace, heart rate,
device name (e.g., "Garmin Forerunner 165"), effort-colored pace line.

**Attribution:** Device name shown inline with run metrics.

**Screenshot:** home-page-last-run.png (attached)

---

### 2. Activity Detail Page — Run Shape Canvas, Metrics, and Splits

**Data displayed:** Effort-colored pace stream visualization with
interactive heart rate, cadence, and grade overlays. Metrics ribbon
showing distance, duration, pace, HR, elevation, cadence. Per-split
breakdown with distance, time, pace, GAP, HR, and cadence.

**Attribution:** "Recorded on Garmin Forerunner 165 via Garmin Connect"
beneath the metrics ribbon. Splits table footer reads "Splits are sourced
from Garmin Connect."

**Screenshots:** activity-detail-canvas.png, activity-detail-metrics.png (attached)

---

### 3. Settings Page — Garmin Connection Status

**Data displayed:** Connected account status, last sync time, device
info, disconnect option, import history.

**Attribution:** "Garmin Connect" heading with Connected badge.

**Screenshot:** settings-garmin.png (attached)

---

## Branding and Attribution Compliance

- All screens displaying Garmin-sourced data include text attribution
  ("Garmin Connect" or "[device name] via Garmin Connect")
- We do NOT modify, stretch, recolor, or distort Garmin logos or marks
- We do NOT suggest Garmin endorses StrideIQ
- We do NOT display competing product advertising on pages showing
  Garmin data
- Garmin data is displayed only to the athlete who owns it
