## Course Challenges

### Newton Hills (Miles 16-21)
- **Heartbreak Hill** at mile 20.5 is NOT the steepest, but comes after 3 prior hills
- Total climb in Newton: ~300 ft over 4 miles
- The "grind" effect is cumulative fatigue, not single hill difficulty

### Net Downhill Concerns
- First 4 miles drop 400+ feet
- **Quad damage** from early downhills affects hill climbing ability later
- Many runners go out too fast due to downhill start
