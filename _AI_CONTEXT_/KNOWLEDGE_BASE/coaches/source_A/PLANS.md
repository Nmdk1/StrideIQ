# Source A - Marathon Training Plans

## Plan Tiers Overview

| Plan | Weekly Peak | Long Run Peak | Target Athlete |
|------|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| Up to 55 mi/wk | 55 miles | 20-21 miles | Recreational competitive |
| 55-70 mi/wk | 70 miles | 22 miles | Serious amateur |
| 70-85 mi/wk | 85 miles | 22-23 miles | Competitive amateur |
| 85+ mi/wk | 100+ miles | 23+ miles | Elite amateur |

## Up to 55 Miles/Week - 18 Week Schedule

### Block 1: Endurance (Weeks 18-13)
**Focus:** Build aerobic base, introduce speed elements

| Week | Volume | Long Run | Key Sessions |
|------|--------|----------|--------------|
| 18 | 42-47 mi | 15 mi | Gen aerobic + strides/hills |
| 17 | 48-54 mi | 17 mi | Marathon-pace run (8 mi @ MP) |
| 16 | 42-46 mi | 14 mi | LT 20-25 min |
| 15 | 44-49 mi | 15 mi | LT 25-30 min |
| 14 | 48-54 mi | 17 mi | Marathon-pace run (10 mi @ MP) |
| 13 | 39-43 mi | 12-13 mi | Recovery week |

### Block 2: LT + Endurance (Weeks 12-9)
**Focus:** Threshold development, maintain endurance

| Week | Volume | Long Run | Key Sessions |
|------|--------|----------|--------------|
| 12 | 44-49 mi | 16 mi | LT 25-30 min |
| 11 | 45-50 mi | 16 mi | LT 30-40 min, VO2max intro |
| 10 | 47-53 mi | 18 mi | Marathon-pace run (12 mi @ MP) |
| 9 | 39-43 mi | 14-15 mi | Recovery week |

### Block 3: Race Preparation (Weeks 8-4)
**Focus:** VO2max sharpening, race-specific work

| Week | Volume | Long Run | Key Sessions |
|------|--------|----------|--------------|
| 8 | 47-52 mi | 17 mi | VO2max 5x1km, LT 30-40 min |
| 7 | 50-56 mi | 19-20 mi | VO2max 5x1km, tune-up race |
| 6 | 51-57 mi | 20-22 mi | Marathon-pace (14 mi @ MP) |
| 5 | 42-47 mi | 17-18 mi | VO2max 5x600m, LT 30 min |
| 4 | 47-52 mi | 19-20 mi | VO2max, tune-up race |

### Block 4: Taper + Race (Weeks 3-1)
**Focus:** Reduce fatigue, maintain fitness

| Week | Volume | Long Run | Key Sessions |
|------|--------|----------|--------------|
| 3 | 41-48 mi | 16-17 mi | VO2max 5x1km, tune-up |
| 2 | 31-35 mi | 12-13 mi | Dress rehearsal (2 mi @ MP) |
| 1 | 16-21 mi | RACE | Easy runs, strides, RACE |

## 55-70 Miles/Week - 18 Week Schedule

Similar structure with higher volumes.
Peak weeks reach 65-70 miles with 20-22 mile long runs.

---

## 70-85 Miles/Week - 18 Week Schedule

Peak weeks reach 80-85+ miles with 22-23 mile long runs.

---

## 85+ Miles/Week - 18 Week Schedule

### Overview
- Starting volume: ~75 miles
- Peak volume: 100-110 miles
- Long runs: Peak at 23+ miles
- Double runs common
- Medium-long runs: 14-16 miles
- Recovery runs: Essential for volume
